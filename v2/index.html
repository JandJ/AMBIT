Export and Save AIM Data

Many of the young people AMBIT is designed for have high levels of Complexity and Comorbidities. The AMBIT AIM assessment can help you rank potentially useful interventions for the problems you have identified with your client. See also WhichInterventionWhen for other advice on how to sequence what to do, or I'm stuck: what next? if you are at a more general impasse.

There are different ways to sort these suggestions, depending on whether you want to look at the whole spread of a young person's difficulties, or to focus on the most severe ones, and whether you want to limit your attention just to the KeyProblems. There is more explanation, at Interactive properties with manualized content.
