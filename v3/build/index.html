AIM-Introduction

Welcome to the AIM

This is a highly interactive assessment, but a 'paper' version is also available to download Here.

What to do:

  1. The practitioner scores this questionnaire, not the young person, though it can be done collaboratively
  2. During the initial assessment, score all 40 items.
    1. You may need to go back to the young person to gather more information.
    2. You should aim to complete within one week of first contact.

01 - Social activities

Young person is involved in non-mandatory organized school or community activities, such as sports, clubs, church groups, etc.
